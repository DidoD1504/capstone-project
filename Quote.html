Dilqn's Quote

This phrase suggests that while hard work is a reliable path to success and personal growth, dreams alone can be deceptive.

Hard work is portrayed as something concrete and dependable, yielding results over time, even if the journey is tough.

On the other hand, dreams are often seen as aspirations that might not always lead to fulfillment, especially if they are not grounded in reality or pursued with the necessary effort.

The message highlights the importance of consistent effort and resilience, implying that hard work will ultimately pay off, while relying solely on dreams might lead to disappointment.
